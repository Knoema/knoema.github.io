Open Data Portal

Enables public sector employees to engage in research and analysis using Knoema's socioeconomic data and tools to facilitate quick and complete data visualization and insight development. The Portal offers a complete data-as-a-service platform that liberates data from its silos for immediate usability. Provides full API access to other data subscriptions to enable one-stop access to all data sources, normalized and tagged in the same manner. It simplifies the entire data lifecycle from capture and collection to distribution and consumption.
